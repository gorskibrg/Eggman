Why 24 eggs a day?

Why am I eating 24 eggs a day versus the Vince Gironda 36 eggs a day method?

Vince Gironda's method of 36 eggs a day consisted of three 12-egg shakes mixed with half&half dranken throughout the day. However, what he did not know back then was that the bioavailability of protein from raw eggs is significantly less than that of cooked eggs. According to this study the bioavailability of protein from raw eggs is around 50%, compared to over 90% from cooked eggs.

Since I am cooking all of my eggs, I am actually getting 25 grams of more protein per day by consuming 66% of what Vince originally suggested. And let's be honest, 24 eggs a day is still ridiculous, let alone 36 eggs a day!

The lesson here is cook all your eggs.
